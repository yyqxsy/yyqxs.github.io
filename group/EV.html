Brief Introduction

Zhang Mingjiang, male, professor, doctoral supervisor. He graduated from The optical engineering major of Tianjin University and was nominated for the national Excellent Doctoral dissertation. He is now the deputy Dean of The School of Physics and Optoelectronic Engineering, Taiyuan University of Technology. Selected young scholars, academic and technical leaders in Shanxi Province, young and middle-aged innovative talents in Colleges and universities in Shanxi Province. He is also a member of optoelectronic Technology and System Integration Branch of China Instrument and Instrument Association, a member of Optoelectronic Technology Committee of China Optical Society, an editorial member of Youth Editorial Committee of China Laser Magazine, and an external expert of Accelink Technologies Co.,Ltd., a state-recognized enterprise technology center. It is devoted to the research of photonic integrated chaotic laser and new distributed fiber sensing technology. He has presided over more than 10 national, provincial and ministerial-level projects, such as the National Major scientific research instrument development project, the General project of the National Natural Science Foundation, the Youth Science Foundation project of the National Natural Science Foundation, the sub-project cooperation project of the 973 Project, and the Shanxi Provincial Science and Technology tackling Key problems project. The chip - level "wideband chaotic laser chip", module - level "photonic integrated semiconductor laser" and device - level "50 GHz wideband chaotic laser source" are developed. Three instruments, high-speed real-time distributed fiber Raman temperature measuring instrument, long-distance and high-precision distributed fiber Raman temperature measuring instrument and high-speed Brillouin distributed fiber strain/temperature measuring instrument, have been developed and applied to the safety monitoring and warning of road tunnels, coal mines and landslides in Shanxi Province. A remote water level sensing technology based on uWB chaotic signal is proposed to solve the difficult problem of water level survey under harsh environment. He has published more than 100 academic papers, 75 of which are included by SCI. A total of 60 patents (25 from the first inventor) have been authorized, including 43 invention patents, 7 utility model patents and 10 software Copyrights. It has won 1 first prize of Technological invention of Shanxi Province (the first accomplisher), 2 second Prize of Natural science of Shanxi Province (the second accomplisher and the third accomplisher), and 2 Technological Invention Award of Shanxi Province (the second accomplisher and the third accomplisher).
